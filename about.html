About

My story

My name is Katica Pranjic, but you can call me Kaya! :)

I am living and working in Munich for the last 6 years, originally coming from beautiful Croatia. After finishing my graphic design high school in Croatia, I was torn between my two biggest passions painting and dancing. That lead me on a journey of self-discovery and exploration. I decided to move to Germany at 22, which is a journey that I am currently still on. After having some unfulfilling jobs and trying to create my own business on the side which didn't work, I really didn't know what to do. Then I found UX and UI, and it clicked with me big time. The industry is progressive and offers a lot of opportunities, it is analytical and creative, and it helps people - basically everything I ever wanted. It fits me perfectly, and I really enjoy learning and working as a designer. I am very motivated and driven to create a professional design career and I believe I have found what I was looking for. When I am not designing you can find me dancing, painting or in nature listening to podcasts and audiobooks from great minds.
